Details and FAQs

...Is this a wedding?

Yes! Think of this celebration as an elopement that we could not have imagined without the presence of our immediate families and closest friends. We have intentionally stripped this day down so we can focus on only that which is essential and most sacred to us.

What are the details for the day's events?

The ceremony will be held at the Colon Township Library in the Smith Room at 3:30. Following the ceremony, we'll provide some snacks and nonalcoholic beverages at the library before heading to Farrand Hall. Guests may start arriving at Farrand Hall at 4:30, where you can enjoy beer, wine, and NA beverages on us. Dinner starts at 6:30 and will be over around 8:30. There's a nonzero chance we cajole everyone into going to the local watering hole known as Curly's after, so if you're staying in Colon for the night, we hope you'll join us!

What is the dress code?

Please keep it casual with us. Our wedding day will be intimate and unconventional, and we want your comfort to be reflected in that. We invite you to join us in prioritizing simplicity over formality. Farrand Hall does not have a dress code and recommends to each guest: "come as you are." Also keep in mind we may be sharing the dinner space with other guests who are not part of our group. Practical shoes are encouraged as you will be walking on grass and gravel, and per the groom's wishes ties are highly discouraged. Seriously, there will be a tie lock box and if you wear one you'll have to relinquish it to us and we'll give it back to you at the end of the night. Maybe.

What's on the menu?

Farrand Hall sets their own menu for Friday night dinners. Menus are usually released a month in advance and can vary from experimental to casual. For those curious you can check out Farrand Hall's website to get excited for the dining experience!

Gifts/registry?

We have all that we need in life and we view your presence on this day as a gift in itself. Your time is truly the most precious thing you can give us. If you feel strongly about giving us a gift, we will accept contributions toward our honeymoon fund (cash, check, venmo, and of course bitcoin). You are also welcome to make a donation to the Colon Township Library in our names.

Do I need to RSVP?

Since we purchased dinner tickets individually ahead of time for each guest, we have likely already been in touch about whether you can make it. We understand that life is unpredictable, though, so if something changes, please let us know!
